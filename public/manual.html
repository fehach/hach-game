HACH GAME

The Official Player's Manual

1 Start the Game

  • Click the big green Play button.
  • The game grabs your mouse so you can look around.
  • Press the Esc key any time to get your mouse back.

2 Move Around

  • W Walk forward
  • S Walk backward
  • A Step left
  • D Step right
  • Space Jump up
  • Mouse Move your mouse to look around

3 Break and Build

  • Left Click to break the block you are looking at.
  • Right Click to build a new block.

A black square shows which block you are pointing at. Get close and aim with the + in the middle of the screen!

4 Pick Your Block

  • Look at the bar at the bottom of the screen.
  • Press number keys 123 ... to choose a block.
  • Or roll the Wheel on your mouse to switch blocks.

5 Be Your Character

  • On the start screen, pick a character like Hero, Ninja, or Dino.
  • Click the + Create tile to make your own character! Pick colors and a name, watch it spin, then save.
  • Press the V key in the game to see yourself from behind.
  • Press V again to go back to first-person view.

Your created characters are saved, so they will be there next time. Grown-ups can also add presets in config/characters.json.

6 Make It Your Own

Want pink grass or a giant tower? You can change the game yourself! Ask a grown-up to open the config folder.

  • blocks.json changes block colors. Try grass color #ff00ff for pink!
  • worlds.json changes the world. Set it to desert or snow.

After you change a file, save it and refresh the game to see the magic.

7 Save Your World

  • Press O to save everything you built.
  • Press L to load your saved world.
  • Press M to turn the sound on or off.

Your world also saves all by itself, so your buildings will be waiting for you next time! Watch the sky too — it slowly turns from day to night.

8 Fun Challenges

  • Build a house with a door you can walk through.
  • Dig a deep tunnel into a hill.
  • Make the tallest tower you can and jump off it.
  • Build a bridge across a valley.
